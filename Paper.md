# Simulation of Consumption vs. Sharing Choices in Limited Resource Economy

Bartosz Bieganowski, Michał Plewnia, Paweł Makuła

## Abstract

We propose a framework of analyzing consumption and sharing decisions using agent-based simulation. The simulation imitates a simple pre-historical economy where resources are foraged and then shared based on decisions made by the agents. We analyze variety of different scenarios and how they impact population dynamics, emergent genetic traits, and survivability of the population.
